About Me

Hello, you guys!!

I'm a 17-year-old producer and songwriter from Germany. I started my musical journey in 2019 when I first discovered GarageBand on the iPad my dad gave me. Being curious, I began exploring the app and its features. After finally understanding how to use it, I started creating my own musical pieces while continuously discovering new possibilities.

At the beginning, I was really interested in trap and hip-hop, so I tried recreating famous songs that were trending at the time, such as The Box by Roddy Ricch and Old Town Road by Lil Nas X.

After some time, my friends found out that I made music, and one of them suggested that I try using FL Studio by Image-Line. After he told me about it, I installed the program and was immediately overwhelmed by all its features. Although the system looked very complicated at first, I kept using it and slowly learned how to work with it.

As soon as I became comfortable with the program, I started using GarageBand less and less. Eventually, I stopped using it completely after buying the Producer Edition of FL Studio because of the limitations of the free version.

During that time, I started picking up new instruments such as guitar and bass. My goal was to learn these instruments so I could make my music sound more interesting and realistic, rather than relying only on plugins for all of my production.
